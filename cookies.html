Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2025

This website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and understand how visitors interact with the site. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can control your cookie preferences.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly and provide information to website owners about how visitors use their site.

How This Website Uses Cookies

This site uses only essential cookies plus one analytics tool:

1. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with the site. Google Analytics collects anonymous data such as:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on each page
  • Browser and device type
  • Approximate geographic region

No personally identifiable information (such as your name, email, or IP address) is stored directly by this site.

Your Cookie Choices

When you visit this website, you may be shown a cookie banner that lets you accept cookies. If you click “Accept,” a small file is stored in your browser so the banner does not appear again.

You can control or delete cookies at any time by:

  • Changing your browser’s privacy settings
  • Clearing your browser’s cookies
  • Using private/incognito mode

Third-Party Cookies

Google Analytics may set third-party cookies to collect traffic data. These cookies are controlled by Google and are subject to Google’s Privacy Policy.

More Information

To learn more about cookies, visit: allaboutcookies.org.
